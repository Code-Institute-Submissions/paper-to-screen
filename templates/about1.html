What we are About

Because many of us definitely don't read as much as we'd like to, the idea of joining a book club seems even more unlikely than happily sharing a pizza (never going to happen). So we created a book club where we can not just read our favorite stories but watch them too. Millions of books have been adapted on the big screen and we are only scratching the surface. We keep tabs on what top leaders are reading or recommending and the beauty of this book club that's not a book club, is that it's online. So no awkward small talk or having to make yourself look presentable to discuss the pages you have absorbed. Book clubs are an opportunity to develop emotional intelligence, foster relationships and improve as communicators. This spoke to me and it spoke to several others around me. so we decided to give it a go. Founded in 2017 we haven't been going for very long but have found great success and some little gems along the way. Even if you never would have picked the selection, a book club challenges you to think differently.
